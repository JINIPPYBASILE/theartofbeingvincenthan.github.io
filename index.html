About

Welcome to The Art of Being Vincent Han Wiki, an official wiki curated to document the story, characters, and trivia within the The Art of Being Vincent Han since 2024.

The Art of Being Vincent Han (also stylized as the art of being VINCENT HAN) is an online webseries/character-based project created and distributed by hobby artist JINIPPYBASILE since 2023.
